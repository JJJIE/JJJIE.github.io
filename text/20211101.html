Guilia Bernardelli, is an Italian lady which makes amazing art with leftover foods such as spilled coffee, breakfast and ice cream too.

Guilia Bernardelli是一位意大利女士，她用洒出来的咖啡、早晨和冰淇淋等剩菜做出了令人惊叹的艺术。

But most of her artwork is done with spilled coffee.

但她的大部分艺术品都是用洒出来的咖啡完成的。

As we know, coffee stains are the really a tough one cleaning job, but this brilliant artistic lady turned messy things into beautiful work of art using spilled liquid and a spoon or whatever else is nearby.

众所周知，清洁咖啡渍是非常艰难的工作，但这位才华横溢的女士利用溢出的液体和勺子或者附近的任何东西，将凌乱的东西变成了美丽的艺术品。

According to her, she never plans creations in advance.

据她说，她从来没有提前计划过创作。

Talking about her unique passion she says, "I decided to replace the brush with what nature offers us, such as leaves, fruit skin or just food".

在谈到她独特的激情时，她说：“我决定用大自然提供给我们的东西来代替刷子，比如树叶，水果皮，或仅仅是食物。

So getting inspired from nature, opposite of other creators who sketch objects with coffee, Bernulia crafted the coffee artworks based on spilled coffee, she edited and give regular coffee splashes a new form by turning into attention-grabbing shapes and pictures.

因此，与其他用咖啡勾画物体的创作者相反，Bernulia从大自然中汲取灵感，根据洒出的咖啡制作艺术品，她编辑并赋予了常规的咖啡飞溅一种新的形势，并将其变成了吸引眼球的形状和图片。

After seeing here artwork with coffee and foods, we bet you will hook on Bernulia's marvelous art.

在看过这里的咖啡和食物艺术品后，我打赌你会喜欢上Bernulia的奇妙艺术。
